What is vibex fitness?

We’re building a modern, performance-driven fitness center in Kottayam with a bold, minimal aesthetic. While construction is underway, we’re finalizing the equipment lineup, training zones, and class schedules so you can hit your goals from day one.

  • Strength & free-weight floor with premium racks and plates
  • Cardio zone with treadmills, bikes, and rowers
  • Functional training & mobility area
  • Locker rooms & shower facilities
  • Personal training & group classes
Under construction. Follow our progress and be first to know about launch dates, early-bird memberships, and opening day events.
